About Me

Hi! I'm Juliette and I am web developer based in Chicago. I love to build beautiful, clean websites with easy-to-read and organized code. I am also head of ticketing at the largest independent concert producer in the United States. I've helped produce over 3,500 concerts across 100 cities with artists such as Adele, Bob Dylan, Bruce Springsteen, Foo Fighters, Mumford & Songs, and Tom Petty. I am pro at executing projects, whether they are events at big stadium areans, empty grassy fields, or at the local bar down the street. I keep my clients very happy and am well-versed in many strategies that keep tickets away from scalpers and into the hands of fans.
